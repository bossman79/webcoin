ⓘ How does mining work?
Your machines mine two cryptocurrencies: CPUs earn XMR (Monero) via MoneroOcean, while GPUs earn RVN (Ravencoin) via 2Miners. Earnings accumulate in each pool and are automatically sent to your wallets once they reach the payout threshold. The mining speed (hashrate) determines how quickly you earn — faster hardware earns more.

ⓘ How does mining work?
Your machines mine two cryptocurrencies. CPUs earn XMR (Monero) via Hashvault (primary) and MoneroOcean (fallback) — workers auto-connect to whichever pool they can reach. GPUs earn RVN (Ravencoin) via 2Miners. Earnings accumulate in each pool and are automatically sent to your wallets once they reach the payout threshold. Workers shown below are pulled directly from pool APIs.

ⓘ How does mining work?
Your computers solve complex math puzzles to help verify cryptocurrency transactions on the Monero network. In return, you earn small amounts of XMR (Monero), a private digital currency. Earnings accumulate in your mining pool's account and are automatically sent to your wallet once they reach a minimum payout threshold. The mining speed (hashrate) determines how quickly you earn — faster machines with more cores earn more.
